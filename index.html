Our Club's Competition and Project Teams

California-Unmanned Aerial Systems Competition

The C-UAS Competition is a new emerging competition that is held by the Mojave Air and Space Port to provide future engineers with an opportunity to learn about Aerial Systems.

Mathworks Minidrone Competition

A yearly competition that allows students to use control and computer vision skills for an autonomous drone. It incorporates the use of simulation and real-life applications.
